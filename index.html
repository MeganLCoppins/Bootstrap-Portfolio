About Me

My name is Megan Coppins and I am currently enrolled in a coding boot camp to pursue a career as a web developer. I grew up in Wisconsin and attended the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee before moving to Phoenix to be closer to family and finish out my degree at ASU. I enjoy hiking, baking, and reading.

I have a B.S. im Nutrition and Dietetics and I have been working in the nutrition field for the last 3 years. I have worked both in a hospital and public health setting. I was working for Maricopa County WIC as a nutrition counselor before making the decision to follow my passion and begin a career in web development.

I am fueled by my passion to solve problems and continuously expand on my knowledge. I am fascinated with learning the many different programming languages and using these languages to take an idea and create useful and efficient web applications.
